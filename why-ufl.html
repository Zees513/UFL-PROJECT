About UFL Pakistan

Delivering excellence in logistics since 2012 with a strong global network, customer-first solutions, and trusted partnerships worldwide.

Who We Are

UFL Pakistan is a leading logistics and freight forwarding company committed to simplifying supply chains with cost-effective and reliable solutions. We specialize in air, sea, road, warehousing, and project forwarding, serving diverse industries with excellence.

With global partnerships and decades of expertise, we ensure security, efficiency, and innovation in every shipment.

Our Leadership

Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Furqan Hussain, UFL Pakistan has grown into a trusted logistics partner for global businesses. With over 13 years of experience in freight forwarding, he has transformed the company with innovation, integrity, and a customer-first approach. His focus on building strong partnerships and delivering sustainable logistics ensures UFL stays ahead in the industry.

Our Success Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a global logistics player, UFL Pakistan has successfully handled thousands of shipments across continents. Our success is built on innovation, trust, and consistent service excellence, making us the logistics partner of choice for businesses worldwide.
